About

A small archive for travel and food writing.

Cassidy Harman is a fictional travel writer for this static site, built around quiet lists, modest typography, and internal archive pages for readers who want the complete set.

Notes from roads, markets, and borrowed desks.

This journal collects dispatches from long bus rides, temporary apartments, morning markets, border towns, and meals shared with people who make a place easier to understand.

The site is intentionally restrained: a narrow reading column, softened contrast, simple post lists, and only the interactions needed for browsing the archives.
